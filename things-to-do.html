scary excitement

Are you fearless? Think again and try our rides below. The only limit is you.

01

The Devil's Eye Roller Coaster

Reaches up to 100 km per hour in 3.2 seconds. Everyone is waiting the queue to get in this ride. It is no doubt to argue that breathtaking experience is ready for you.

02

The G Shock Roller Coaster

You do not need to be a pilot in order to experience G force. Here, we prepared a ride for you. It peaks up to half kilometers in 5 seconds and drops you off. Of course with security! You will experience ten turns around yourself at least.
