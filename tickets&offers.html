Covid Caution

We always update our health and safety measures for risks related to COVID-19. Please note that it is required for our guests to wear masks for common ground, during the rides and in concerts. The area is equipped with hygiene masks and liquids. We reduced the capacity for each event.

Traffic instruction

There are always traffic on weekends and we urge our guests to come early. We are just 10 minutes away form the main marina. Contact us for more details.

Parking Info

Parking is free for all visitors. There are VALE parking option too. We have 5000 car capacity in our huge parking area.

Ticket Return Policy

Tickets bought online can be returned in 48 hours. If you enter the amusement park, we do not accept returned tickets.

Fast Pass

You may wish to buy fast pass tickets to avoid longer queues on each ride. Please get detailed information for fast pass tickets from our customer relations office.

Disabled Policy

Accessibility is one of the most important topics for our guests. We commit to offer best experience for our guests. We offer responsible helpers for our guests who need wheelchair. Our helpers assist you all the day.
